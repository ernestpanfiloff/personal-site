This upbeat satirical musical spins a Yemeni - Saudi love story against the backdrop of corruption in the international arms trade.

We are presenting the second workshop for this musical in early development stage at The Other Palace, Victoria, on 28 April at 3pm. We are proud of the progress so far and hope to find further support, friends and partners for the project.

There will be refreshments and forty minutes of material on its feet, including several songs and dances.

The cast comprises excellent rising musicians and acting talent alongside established performers like Waleed Elgadi, David Mumeni, Julie Jupp and Yasmeen Audi.

The project boasts Hassan Abdulrazzak behind the book and KS Lewkowicz as composer, with Rosamunde Hutt directing, Candida Caldicot as MD, and Anjali Mehra Hughes as Choreographer. Please see below for more info on the creative team.
